System Integration/Senior Integration Analyst - AITS (Hybrid/Remote)

Duties:
1.Provide expertise for selecting and implementing services, understanding both business processes and implementation methods.
2.Lead integration projects, monitor progress, and ensure milestones are met.
3.Design complex integrations across multiple systems to deliver complete business solutions.
4.Analyze business requirements, document integration components, and develop test suites.
5.Ensure appropriate technical resources are allocated and support integration work.
6.Identify and implement improvements to enhance operations and resolve errors.
7.Support integrations in both production and non-production environments.
8.Research solutions and industry practices, provide estimates, and resolve customer complaints.
9.Collaborate with team members, receive coaching and mentoring as needed.
10.Participate in information sharing and present proposed changes for architectural review.
11.Communicate and enforce standards for network, computer, and data security.
12.Provide off-hours/on-call support when required.
